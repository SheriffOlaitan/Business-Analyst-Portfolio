## Introduction & Business Need

MediConnect is developing a Clinic Appointment & Patient Management System (CAPMS) to help small private clinics digitize their operations. The system addresses the need for efficient appointment booking, automated reminders, streamlined schedule management, and secure patient record storage.

### Business Objectives

- Reduce no-show rates by 30% through automated reminders
- Improve clinic operational efficiency by 40%
- Enhance patient satisfaction and experience
- Digitize patient records for secure and easy access
- Streamline billing and payment processes

### Process flow

### As-Is Process: Patient Appointment Booking

1. Patient calls clinic or visits in person
2. Receptionist checks physical appointment book
3. Receptionist manually searches for available time slots
4. Patient verbally provides personal and medical information
5. Receptionist manually records appointment details
6. Receptionist provides verbal confirmation
7. No automated reminder system
8. High chance of no-shows and scheduling conflicts

### To-Be Process: Patient Appointment Booking 

1. Patient visits CAPMS website/mobile app
2. Patient logs in or registers account
3. System displays available doctors and specialties
4. Patient selects preferred doctor and views available time slots
5. Patient books appointment with one click
6. System sends instant confirmation via email/SMS
7. System sends automated reminder 24 hours before appointment
8. Patient can reschedule or cancel online if needed
