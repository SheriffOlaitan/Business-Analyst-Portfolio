### Patient Module

- Online appointment booking with available time slots
- View and manage appointments (reschedule/cancel)
- Access medical records and consultation history
- Receive notifications (SMS/Email reminders)
- Patient profile management
- View billing and payment history

### 3.2 Doctor Module

- View and manage daily schedule
- Set availability and working hours
- Add consultation notes and prescriptions
- Access patient medical history
- Manage appointment requests (accept/reject)
- View patient queue in real-time

### 3.3 Admin Module

- User management (add/remove doctors, staff, patients)
- Billing and invoice generation
- Payment tracking and reconciliation
- System reporting and analytics
- Configuration and settings management
- Audit logs and compliance tracking
